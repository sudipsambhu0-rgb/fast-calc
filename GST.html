What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services in India. It replaced multiple taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty.

How to Use This GST Calculator

  • Enter the base amount
  • Select the GST rate (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%)
  • Choose whether to add or remove GST
  • Click calculate to get GST amount and final price

GST Formula

GST Amount = (Original Amount × GST Rate) / 100

For Adding GST

Final Price = Amount + GST

For Removing GST

Base Price = Amount / (1 + GST/100)

Example Calculation

Amount = ₹1000

GST Rate = 18%

GST = ₹180

Why GST is Important

GST ensures transparency in taxation and creates a unified tax system across India. It helps businesses manage taxes efficiently.
