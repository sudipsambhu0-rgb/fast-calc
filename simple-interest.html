What is Simple Interest?

Simple interest is a basic method of calculating the interest charged or earned on a loan or investment. It is calculated only on the principal amount and does not include interest on previously earned interest. The formula for simple interest is: Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) ÷ 100.

This calculator helps you quickly determine how much interest you will earn or pay over a period of time. It is useful for students, lenders, borrowers, and anyone dealing with financial planning.

Simple interest is commonly used in short-term loans, personal lending, and basic financial calculations. It is easy to understand and does not involve complex compounding formulas.

To use this tool, enter the principal amount, interest rate, and time duration. Click calculate and the calculator will show both the interest amount and the total payable amount instantly.

Understanding simple interest helps you make better financial decisions and compare loan options easily.
