What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement that uses your height and weight to estimate if your body weight is healthy. It is widely used by doctors and health experts.

How to Use This BMI Calculator

  • Enter your weight in kilograms (kg)
  • Enter your height in centimeters (cm)
  • Click on "Calculate BMI"
  • You will get your BMI value and category instantly

BMI Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9 – Normal weight
  • 25 – 29.9 – Overweight
  • 30 and above – Obese

Example Calculation

If your weight is 70kg and height is 170cm (1.7m):

BMI = 70 / (1.7 × 1.7) = 24.22

Why BMI Matters

BMI helps you understand your health risks. Being overweight or underweight can increase the chances of heart disease, diabetes, and other health conditions.

Important Note
