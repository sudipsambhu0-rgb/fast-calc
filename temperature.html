What is a Temperature Calculator?

A temperature calculator is a tool that helps you convert temperature values between different units. The three most commonly used temperature scales are Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin. Each scale is used in different parts of the world and in different fields of science.

Celsius is the most widely used scale in everyday life across the world. Water freezes at 0 degrees Celsius and boils at 100 degrees Celsius. Fahrenheit is commonly used in the United States. In Fahrenheit, water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees.

Kelvin is used in science and physics. It starts from absolute zero, which is the lowest possible temperature. Zero Kelvin is equal to minus 273.15 degrees Celsius. Kelvin has no negative values, which makes it useful in scientific calculations.

This calculator supports all six conversions between these three scales. You just need to select the conversion type, enter the value, and click the convert button. The result will be shown instantly with two decimal precision.

Temperature conversion is used in cooking, weather forecasting, chemistry, and engineering. Knowing how to convert between units helps you understand temperature values from different sources around the world.
