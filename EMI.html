What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest.

How to Use This EMI Calculator

  • Enter your total loan amount
  • Enter annual interest rate
  • Enter loan duration in years
  • Click calculate to get your monthly EMI

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × r × (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n − 1]

Where:

  • P = Loan amount
  • r = Monthly interest rate
  • n = Number of months

Why EMI Planning is Important

Understanding your EMI helps you manage your finances better, avoid loan stress, and plan your savings efficiently.

Tips to Reduce EMI

  • Choose longer tenure
  • Make higher down payment
  • Look for lower interest rates
  • Prepay loan when possible
