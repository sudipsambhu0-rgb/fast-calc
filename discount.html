What is a Discount Calculator?

A discount calculator is a simple tool used to calculate the final price of a product after applying a percentage discount. It helps shoppers quickly determine how much money they save during sales, offers, or promotional deals.

How to Calculate Discount?

The formula to calculate discount is:

Discount Amount = Original Price × Discount % ÷ 100

Final Price = Original Price − Discount Amount

Example

If a product costs ₹1000 and has a 20% discount:
Discount = ₹200 Final Price = ₹800

Where is Discount Calculator Useful?

• Online shopping
• Festival sales
• Supermarket offers
• Business pricing decisions
• Budget planning
